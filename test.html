Misfit Union

Package Copy

An edgy brand story for a maverick start-up on a mission to change the way people buy and consume food. Package design by RTD.

“Way to go, misfit. You’ve just saved the planet 9 truck trips and made a local farmer happy. And you’re eating good, clean, hand-prepared food that doesn’t come with any ingredients you can’t pronounce. So happy, healthy eating. Enjoy every ounce of this nutrition-packed meal before you recycle our eco-package. Try a new combi­nation every time. And, please, you keep doing you. Don’t fit in. Join the evolution.”

Skills

Content and UX Writing

I’m really good at hooking readers with snappy, absorbing content at any length, in any style. I have a soft spot for writing articles and designing content for websites, but I’m just as at home with marketing emails, video scripts, product copy, Google ads, and more. (That “more” includes SEO titles and descriptions.)

Creative Direction

I love coming up with memorable ideas that can spark marketing and social media cam­paigns, and even brand identities. And I love working with passionate teams to make those ideas happen.

Storytelling

My background is in fiction and journalism, so telling company stories is second nature. I also teach creative writing and have written extensively about story­telling for the web, which comes in very handy when I need to communicate my creative choices to the teams I work with.

Visual Thinking

I’m fluent in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop (and now Affinity Designer and Photo), which I use to create professional photo illustrations and collages for my work. I’m also a website designer, and occasionally have the chance to design the websites I write. All of which is to say that I never write a word without imagining how it will look on the page.
